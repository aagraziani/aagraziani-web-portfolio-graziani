About me

Hey there! My name is Agustin and I'm a graphic designer with over 9 years of experience, all of it spent at the most prominent economic newspaper in Argentina. With no prior experience, I jumped into the deep end and ended up doing a great job, including participating in the newspaper's redesign and creating the new look and feel for its social media.
Recently, I had the opportunity to work at PwC in the IT area, specifically in Identity and Access Management, another area where I had no prior experience. And guess what? I'm once again doing a great job!

Now, here's the truth: I don't have experience working as a programmer in a company... but hey, we all have to start somewhere! I've been developing personal projects that have helped me sharpen my skills. So, while my resume might not be packed with tech job titles, I've got a ton of drive and a proven track record of making things happen when I set my mind to it. Ready to take on this adventure together?
